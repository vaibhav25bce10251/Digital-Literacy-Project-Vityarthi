# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, bank password, or debit card details with anyone.
2. Remember that receiving money does not require entering your UPI PIN.
3. Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people, especially when they claim it is needed to receive payment.
4. Verify every payment request carefully before approving it in your UPI app.
5. Avoid clicking suspicious banking or payment-related links received through SMS, email, or messaging apps.
6. Do not install unknown apps or screen-sharing apps at someone else’s instruction.
7. Check the identity of the sender before trusting any message related to bank accounts, refunds, or rewards.
8. Use a strong phone lock and enable biometric or app lock protection for payment apps.
9. Regularly review your bank statements and transaction history for unusual activity.
10. Keep your mobile number, payment apps, and device software updated and secure.
11. If money is lost in an online fraud, report it immediately to your bank and payment service provider.
12. Report cybercrime through the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
13. For urgent financial fraud complaints, call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
