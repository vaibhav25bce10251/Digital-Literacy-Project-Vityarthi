# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
1. Use respectful and polite language in posts and comments.
2. Verify information before sharing it online.
3. Protect your personal information and privacy settings.
4. Share useful, positive, and professional content.
5. Think carefully before posting anything publicly.

## Don’ts
1. Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
2. Do not share fake news or unverified information.
3. Do not reveal passwords, OTPs, or sensitive details.
4. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
5. Do not upload content that may damage your academic or professional image.
