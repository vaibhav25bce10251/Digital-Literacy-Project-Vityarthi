## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

My name is Vaibhav Tiwari, and I am a first-year (2025) B.Tech CSE student at VIT Bhopal University.

For Task 1, I created a one-page infographic on **Digital Literacy for Students**. The infographic explains the meaning of digital literacy and why it is important for college students in their academic and personal lives.

The design covers the following topics:
- What digital literacy means
- Useful digital tools for students
- Safe internet practices
- Professional online presence

I created the infographic using **Canva** and designed it in a simple and visually clear format so that it can be easily understood by first-year students. The infographic highlights common digital tools such as Google Docs, Google Drive, GitHub, and Canva. It also includes basic online safety tips like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and being careful about what we post online.

This task helped me understand that digital literacy is not only about using technology, but also about using it responsibly, safely, and effectively.
